Common Mental Health Issues

Anxiety

Excessive worry about academic performance, social situations, or future career.

Suggested Solutions:

  • Practice mindfulness and deep breathing exercises
  • Maintain a regular exercise routine
  • Seek counseling support

Depression

Persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest, and difficulty concentrating.

Suggested Solutions:

  • Establish a daily routine
  • Connect with friends and family
  • Professional counseling and support

Academic Burnout

Exhaustion from academic pressure, leading to decreased motivation and performance.

Suggested Solutions:

  • Break tasks into smaller, manageable parts
  • Take regular study breaks
  • Practice stress management techniques
